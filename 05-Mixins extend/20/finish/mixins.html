About Pie in the Sky Bakery

Pie in the Sky Bakery is your specialty pie shop. We make all our products fresh daily right here in our own kitchen. Stop by for a slice of your favorite pie, or take a pie home to enjoy with your family and friends. Need pies for a special event? See our Catering page for ordering details!

In 2006, Pie in the Sky Bakery relocated from Merilee Wilbur's kitchen to the current location at 34 Main Street, much to the delight of the Springdale community.

Pie in the Sky Bakery is proud to partner with the Culinary Arts program of Springdale Technical High School by providing internships for STHS students right here in our local community.
